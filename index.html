About me




This project is a concept for a turn-based RPG game, where the player explores dungeons composed of multiple floors and rooms. Each room offers access to two others, allowing the player to choose their path after each battle or event. The rooms are numbered, like 'Room 4, 5, 10', and each floor has a specific number of rooms as shown in the image.

The project is influenced by Buriedborne, Albion Online, and Dark and Darker. This high-risk MMO turn-based RPG severely penalizes player defeat, resulting in the loss of all their items.

Rooms and Floors

Each room will present random events, including encounters with enemies, NPC appearances, or discoveries of special attributes.

Random events, such as finding a mysterious waterfall, offer choices to the player that are influenced by their attributes, potentially resulting in positive or negative consequences. Some events can be ignored by the player.

Room attributes

The room attributes include characteristics such as 'Illuminated' (the player can see the contents of the next rooms), 'Narrow' (the player cannot dodge), 'Rest' (the player recovers health), and 'Portal' (passage to somewhere random within the floor), among others.

If a player is defeated in a specific room, they become an 'NPC enemy' with a 20% chance of appearing to other players who pass through the same room. This adds an extra layer of challenge and interactivity to the game. It is also possible for the player to choose to keep their character on 'Guard' in the room, and the next players who pass through that room will have a 20% chance of encountering and fighting them.

Combat is turn-based, with the player and the enemy alternating actions. Some skills or attributes can grant additional turns. The attributes of each class or character influence the damage and other capabilities in combat.

The inventory ('Backpack') can be accessed at any time outside of combat, containing the items the player has collected. It has not yet been decided if there will be an item limit.



The Bank is a shared inventory for all characters where they can store items from past adventures.



If the player is defeated, only the equipment in the backpack will be lost.
